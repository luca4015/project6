Planning and Design

Construction is the process of building or assembling infrastructure, buildings, or other physical structures. It involves a wide range of activities, from the initial planning and design stages to the final construction and maintenance of a project.

  • This type of construction involves building homes, apartments, townhouses, and condominiums.
  • These projects require specific zoning, accessibility standards, and safety regulations
  • Permits and inspections are necessary at various stages.

Construction is a multifaceted process that involves a combination of planning, design, engineering, labor, and coordination. Whether building homes, offices, or large infrastructure projects, each step of construction is vital to ensuring a successful and sustainable outcome.
